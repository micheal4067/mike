About Ticket

"Ticket is a software web application solution designed for issuing tickets to vehicles at a park. The Ticket App enables users to customize their enterprise name and set fees for different vehicle types. Upon clicking each vehicle button, a receipt is generated for printing, including the fee, date, and time. When users log in, the system records and displays their login time and date on the dashboard. Additionally, users can view their sales history and print a receipt for daily sales, which includes the total sales amount, login time and date, and the receipt print time and date. Upon logging out, all sales history and saved data are deleted."
